OrangeChicken

What is it

Fried chicken, coated in a sweet soy orange glaze. Sounds easy enough to me... But Can we beat Panda Express?

Ingredients

Chicken Dredge

  • 1.5# Chicken
  • 1 egg
  • salt
  • msg
  • corn starch
  • 1/2 cup flour

Sauce

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • zest/juice of 1 orange
  • 1tbsp cornstarch
  • 4 cloves garlic, grated
  • 2 inch knob ginger, grated
  • 1-2 fresno chillies

Instructions

Sauce Method

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, white vinegar, soy sauce, water, and zest and juice of an orange. Whisk together and bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. Reduce to medium heat once boiling and let simmer while optionally adding saixing wine and chinkian vinegar. Simmer for 8-10 minutes.
  3. Separately mix together cornstarch and water then whisk into the hot sauce for an additional minute or thickened.
  4. Immediately add grated garlic and ginger, and finely minced fresno chilies. Whisk and let steep.

Chicken Method

  1. Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces and lightly season with salt.
  2. In a medium size bowl, whisk together cornstarch and all-purpose flour.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs with a splash of water.
  4. Toss the chicken pieces into the flour mixture then egg mixture, and then back into the flour mixture. Place to the side and rinse and repeat.
  5. Fill a heavy bottom pot with frying oil 2-3‚Äù high and heat to 375F.
  6. Fry chicken in batches for 3-5 minutes and cool on a wire rack.
  7. Toss chicken and the sauce in a bowl until fully coated.
  8. Serve topped with green onions and sliced fresh fresno chilis
